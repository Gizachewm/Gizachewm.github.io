Entry Visa and Residence Permit Information

Click the Link Below to Download Information Regarding the Entry Visa and Residence Permit

Students enrolled at University of Gondar should enter Ethiopia with a Student Visa from the Embassy/Consular Office of Ethiopia in their country of residence. Students coming from countries where there are no Ethiopian Embassies/Consular Offices will get a Visa on Arrival through the Office of International Relations (OIR).
